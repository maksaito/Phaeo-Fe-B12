MA Plot¶

An M-A plot is a comparison between two treatments A & B

x-axis: log2 average normalized spectral counts of treatments A & B y-axis: log2 fold change in normalized spectral counts of treatments A & B

Calculate Fold Change¶

  1. Calculate mean of A & B
  2. Calculate log2 fold change of A and B
